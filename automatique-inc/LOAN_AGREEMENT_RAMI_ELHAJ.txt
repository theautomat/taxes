BETWEEN:

    BORROWER:
    Automatique Inc, a Delaware C-Corporation
    Represented by: Kurt Braget, Chief Executive Officer

================================================================================
RECITALS
================================================================================

WHEREAS, the Lender provided funds to the Borrower for the purpose of operating
capital to support business operations; and

WHEREAS, the Parties wish to memorialize the terms of this loan and establish
a repayment schedule;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements set
forth herein, the Parties agree as follows:


================================================================================
ARTICLE 1: LOAN TERMS
================================================================================

1.1 PRINCIPAL AMOUNT
    The principal amount of this loan is Twenty Thousand Dollars ($20,000.00 USD).

1.2 DATE OF FUNDING
    The Lender transferred the principal amount to the Borrower on June 14, 2024,
    via wire transfer to the Company's Mercury business checking account.

1.3 PURPOSE OF LOAN
    The loan was made for the express purpose of providing operating capital
    to support the Company's business operations, including but not limited to
    software development, contractor payments, hosting, and general operational
    expenses.

1.4 ACKNOWLEDGMENT OF RECEIPT
    The Borrower acknowledges receipt of the full principal amount on the
    Date of Funding specified above.


================================================================================
ARTICLE 2: REPAYMENT TERMS
================================================================================

2.1 REPAYMENT SCHEDULE
    The Borrower agrees to repay the principal amount in eight (8) equal
    monthly installments of Two Thousand Five Hundred Dollars ($2,500.00 USD)
    each, according to the following schedule:

    Payment #   Due Date          Amount
    ---------   ---------------   ----------
    1           February 28, 2026   $2,500.00
    2           March 31, 2026      $2,500.00
    3           April 30, 2026      $2,500.00
    4           May 31, 2026        $2,500.00
    5           June 30, 2026       $2,500.00
    6           July 31, 2026       $2,500.00
    7           August 31, 2026     $2,500.00
    8           September 30, 2026  $2,500.00
                                   ----------
    TOTAL                          $20,000.00

2.2 SOURCE OF REPAYMENT
    Repayment shall be made from Company profits and operating funds. The
    Borrower's obligation to repay is not contingent upon profitability;
    however, the Parties acknowledge that Company profits are the intended
    source of repayment funds.

2.3 PAYMENT METHOD
    Payments shall be made via bank transfer, check, or other mutually
    agreed-upon method to an account designated by the Lender.

2.4 PREPAYMENT
    The Borrower may prepay any portion of the outstanding principal at any
    time without penalty.


================================================================================
ARTICLE 3: INTEREST
================================================================================

3.1 INTEREST RATE
    This loan shall bear simple interest at a rate of Five Percent (5%) per
    annum on any outstanding balance that remains unpaid after its scheduled
    due date.

3.2 INTEREST CALCULATION
    Interest on late payments shall accrue daily on the unpaid balance from
    the scheduled due date until the date of actual payment. Interest shall
    be calculated as follows:

    Daily Interest = (Outstanding Balance) x (5% / 365)

3.3 NO INTEREST ON TIMELY PAYMENTS
    No interest shall accrue on payments made on or before their scheduled
    due dates. This is a zero-interest loan provided all payments are made
    according to the schedule in Section 2.1.

3.4 INTEREST PAYMENT
    Any accrued interest shall be due and payable along with the final
    principal payment or upon full repayment of the loan, whichever occurs
    first.


================================================================================
ARTICLE 4: DEFAULT
================================================================================

4.1 EVENTS OF DEFAULT
    The following shall constitute Events of Default under this Agreement:

    (a) Failure to make any scheduled payment within thirty (30) days of
        its due date;

    (b) The Borrower files for bankruptcy, becomes insolvent, or makes an
        assignment for the benefit of creditors;

    (c) The Borrower ceases business operations;

    (d) Any material breach of this Agreement that remains uncured for
        thirty (30) days after written notice.

4.2 REMEDIES UPON DEFAULT
    Upon an Event of Default, the Lender may, at their option:

    (a) Declare the entire unpaid principal balance, plus any accrued
        interest, immediately due and payable;

    (b) Pursue any legal remedies available under applicable law.

4.3 CURE PERIOD
    The Borrower shall have thirty (30) days from written notice of default
    to cure any payment default before the Lender may exercise remedies
    under Section 4.2.

6.2 AMENDMENTS

6.3 GOVERNING LAW

    the laws of the State of Delaware, without regard to its conflict of

6.4 SEVERABILITY

6.5 NOTICES

    shall have no equity interest, ownership stake, or voting rights in

================================================================================

    The Parties acknowledge that this loan was made at arm's length and

7.2 TAX REPORTING
    Each Party is responsible for their own tax reporting obligations

7.3 DOCUMENTATION
    The Parties agree to maintain adequate records and documentation of
